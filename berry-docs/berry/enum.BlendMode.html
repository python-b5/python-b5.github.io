Enum BlendMode

A blend mode for drawing.

Can either be a preset mode (probably sufficient most of the time), or a custom mode composed of individual BlendFactor and BlendOperation values.

scoped enum BlendMode
{
    Normal,
    Add,
    Subtract,
    SubtractInv,
    Multiply,
    Minimum,
    Maximum,
    Custom(BlendFactor, BlendFactor, BlendOperation, BlendFactor, BlendFactor, BlendOperation)
}
